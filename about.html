What is Hack Club ?

Hack Club is a global network of high school coding clubs, where students learn to code and build projects together. Many projects serve as a way to link people from all over the world together and thrive for more knowledge and to improve!
But don't be fooled, there are projects for everyone, no matter their level or knowledge!
